About Us

Who We Are

Founded by passionate cricketers, Seam Cricket Academy is built on a vision to create a dynamic environment where talent is nurtured, technique is honed, and confidence is built. With experienced coaches and a strong emphasis on personal attention, we believe in developing well-rounded cricketers.

Our Philosophy

At Seam, we go beyond just technique. We instill discipline, focus, and a love for the game in each student creating not just better players, but better sportspeople.
